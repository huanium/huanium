Huân Q. Bùi

I am a second-year student studying Physics and Mathematics with a Statistics minor at Colby College. I am currently working as a math and physics tutor, teaching assistant for Modern Physics and Linear Algebra, and research assistant at Conover lab.

A Quick Guide to... is a series of introductory courses on a few selected physics and math topics that I compile from my lecture notes. The guides assume little familiarity with the subject matter, and are aimed towards undergraduates or enthusiasts alike. Please feel free to take a look or use them as revision resources!

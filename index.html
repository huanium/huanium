Huân Q. Bùi

I'm a second-year student of Physics and Mathematics with a Statistics minor at Colby College in Waterville, Maine, USA. I'm working as a research assistant for Professor Charles Conover on atomic physics here at Colby. I have been a teaching assistant for Modern Physics and Linear Algebra. Next fall, I will be TA-ing for Ordinary Differential Equations.

A Quick Guide to... is a series of selected physics and mathematics introductory short courses compiled from my lecture notes. The guides assume little familiarity with the subject matter, and are aimed towards undergraduates or enthusiasts alike. Please feel free to take a look or use them as revision resources.

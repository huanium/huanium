Huân Q. Bùi

I am a third-year student of Physics and Mathematics with a Statistics minor at Colby College in Waterville, Maine, USA. I am currently in Steven Rolston's group at the Joint Quantum Institute (JQI) this summer working on AMO physics experiments with optical nanofibers. During the school year, I work in Professor Charles Conover's lab studying Rydberg atoms. At Colby, I have not always been a math and physics TA. I also don't always tutor math or physics on the weekends. However, I am always down for ultimate frisbee practices.

A Quick Guide to is a series of selected physics and math short courses compiled from my lecture and reading notes. The guides assume little familiarity with the subject and are aimed towards undergraduates or enthusiasts. Please feel free to have a look.
